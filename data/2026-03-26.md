This lack of spectral or timing variations with orbital and superorbital phases suggests that the mechanisms driving the observed superorbital modulation and pulse period changes are likely associated with large-scale stellar wind structures, such as Co-Rotating Interaction regions, within the stellar wind of the supergiant companion.
